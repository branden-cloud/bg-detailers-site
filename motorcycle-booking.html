Before You Book

Common questions from riders.

A few quick answers to make the booking process feel easier before you continue to scheduling.

Which package is best for a first appointment?

Premium Motorcycle Detail is usually the strongest starting point if you want the best balance of clean, finish, and overall presentation.

Can I add protection later?

Yes. You can start with a strong cleaning package and add protection when it makes the most sense for your bike and riding habits.

What if the bike is heavily used or has buildup?

Heavy road grime or extra buildup may require an adjustment or add-on depending on the condition at the time of service.
