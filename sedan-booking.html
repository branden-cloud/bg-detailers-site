Before You Book

Common questions from sedan owners.

A few quick answers to make the booking process feel easier before you move to the final scheduling step.

Do you come to my location?

Yes. B&G Detail is built around mobile convenience, so service can be performed at your home or workplace depending on the appointment.

Which package is best for a first appointment?

Full Detail is usually the best starting point if the vehicle needs a complete reset. Interior or Exterior Detail works well if your focus is more specific.

What if my sedan is dirtier than average?

Heavy buildup, pet hair, extra items, or unusual condition may require an add-on or a small pricing adjustment at the time of service.
